Splyto Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Splyto (package id com.tripsplit.app.tripsplit) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, nickname, email, encrypted password hash.
  • Trip data: trips, members, expenses, settlements, and related notes.
  • Uploaded content: receipt images, trip images, profile avatar.
  • Device/app information: app version, platform, basic diagnostics, push token for notifications.

Permissions

Splyto may request the following permissions:

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): used for QR code scanning and taking photos (for receipts/profile).
  • Photos/Media: used to select and upload images.
  • Notifications: used to deliver trip and payment updates.

How We Use Information

  • To provide trip expense splitting features.
  • To sync your data across devices and sessions.
  • To send notifications related to your trips.
  • To improve app reliability, performance, and security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only as needed to operate the service (for example, hosting, database, and push delivery providers), and only for service functionality.

Data Retention

We keep account and trip data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

Splyto is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can update account details in the app profile settings.
  • You can manage app permissions in your device settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact: info@egm.lv
